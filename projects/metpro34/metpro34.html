MetPro34

Project Info:

The most powerful metronome on the market, MetPro34 is the easiest way to organize your metronome sequences by organization. It is incredibly easy to program and rehearse any music with changing time signatures, beat settings, and subdivisions.

Project Details:

  • Status:Beta
  • Industry:Music
  • Technologies:React.js, Express.js, Redux, Swift, Android, Mailgun API, Stripe API, MongoDB, Google Cloud, Google Analytics
  • URL:www.metpro34.com

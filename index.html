About Me

About Me

My name's Anthony D'Andrea.

Ever since my first "Hello, World!" on my TI-84 in high school, I've been captivated by the idea of creating software that is used by real people. Given a problem, I enjoy working to find a solution that is both highly useful and easy to use.

Computers aside, I'm a passionate musician, specifically a percussionist. I've performed at the World Class level for Marching Percussion, and currently enjoy teaching both at the top level as well as at high school programs. I also like arranging Battery percussion music.

In my free time, when I'm not coding or drumming, you'll typically find me weightlifting, running, cooking, or gaming with friends (I'm bad, but I boost team morale). I also occassionally like to golf and play pool.
